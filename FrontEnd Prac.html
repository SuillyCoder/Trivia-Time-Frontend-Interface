DID YOU KNOW...

Crocodiles are often referred to as "living fossils" because they have changed very little in the last 200 million years. They’re also incredibly powerful swimmers, being able to swim at 20 miles per hour (32 kilometers per hour) and leap out of the water with astonishing agility. Lastly, they have a unique way of cooling down, wherein they often open their mouths to release heat, which is why you might see them basking with their mouths wide open.

DID YOU KNOW...

The Great Pyramid of Giza, built for Pharaoh Khufu, is the largest of the Egyptian pyramids and one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, standing at 138.8 meters (455 feet). Its construction remains a mystery, but it’s believed that they were built using a large labor force of skilled workers and advanced engineering techniques (such as leveling systems and precise measurements).This served as a perfect exhibition of the Egyptians’ profound understanding of astronomy and mathematics.

Visual Studio Code (VS Code) was developed by Microsoft, released in April 2015, and quickly gained popularity among developers. It’s built using Electron, which is a framework for developing cross-platform desktop applications using web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. One of the key features that sets VS Code apart is its extension marketplace, which offers a vast library of extensions that cater to a wide range of programming languages and development tools.
